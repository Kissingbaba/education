About Us

Samba Essa Institution is an Islamic Online Educational platform which was established in 2020 by Imam Shaf'i Sankanu. The platform provides many islamic courses with certificate of completion after any course, staff members build rapport with their students. Learning is done online and live through any convenient media like zoom, skype, whatsApp and so on depending on the students condition.
